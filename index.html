About MVGR College of Engineering

Maharaj Vijayaram Gajapathi Raj College of Engineering (MVGRCE) was established in 1997 and is located in Chintalavalasa, near Vizianagaram, Andhra Pradesh, India. The college is situated in a picturesque environment conducive to academic pursuits.

Accreditation:

MVGRCE is affiliated with Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Kakinada (JNTUK) and is recognized by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE). The institution has received accreditation from the National Board of Accreditation (NBA) for several of its programs.

Programs Offered:

MVGRCE offers a range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various engineering disciplines. Key undergraduate programs include:

  • B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering
  • B.Tech in Electronics and Communication Engineering
  • B.Tech in Electrical and Electronics Engineering
  • B.Tech in Civil Engineering
  • B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering
  • B.Tech in Information Technology

Postgraduate programs (M.Tech) are available in specialized fields, focusing on advanced engineering concepts and research.

Campus and Facilities:

The college boasts a sprawling campus equipped with modern facilities, including well-equipped laboratories, libraries, and hostels. It also features sports and recreational facilities to promote the overall development of students.

Research and Innovation:

MVGRCE encourages a strong research culture, promoting innovation and development through various projects and collaborations with industry partners. Students are actively involved in research activities, workshops, and seminars.

Student Development:

The college emphasizes holistic development through extracurricular activities, skill development programs, and leadership training. Various clubs and organizations foster student engagement and community service.

Placement Opportunities:

MVGRCE has a dedicated placement cell that facilitates job placements for graduates. The college has a strong track record of successful placements in reputed companies across various sectors.

Academics at MVGR College of Engineering

Maharaj Vijayaram Gajapathi Raj College of Engineering (MVGRCE) offers a variety of undergraduate and postgraduate programs aimed at fostering technical excellence and innovative thinking among students.

Undergraduate Programs:

  • Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech): Various specializations are available, including:
    • Computer Science and Engineering
    • Electronics and Communication Engineering
    • Electrical and Electronics Engineering
    • Civil Engineering
    • Mechanical Engineering
    • Information Technology

The B.Tech program is designed to provide a solid foundation in engineering principles, coupled with practical training.

Postgraduate Programs:

  • Master of Technology (M.Tech): Specializations include:
    • Computer Science and Engineering
    • Structural Engineering
    • VLSI Design
    • Thermal Engineering

These programs focus on advanced technical knowledge and research skills, preparing students for higher-level positions in the industry or academia.

Research and Development:

MVGRCE promotes a strong culture of research, encouraging students and faculty to engage in innovative projects. Various research centers and labs are available to facilitate this.

Industry Collaboration:

The college collaborates with industries to ensure that the curriculum stays relevant to current industry standards, providing students with practical exposure through internships and projects.

Student Development:

Emphasis is placed on holistic development through co-curricular and extracurricular activities, including workshops, seminars, and technical fests.
